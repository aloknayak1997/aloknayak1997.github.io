About Us

#Health is Wealth – Keep this treasure Safe

Fitter, healthier, happier

Headquartered in Mumbai, sensebio is a healthtech startup which can track and monitor overall health, fitness, and food information of an individual and family, that "sparks people" to live healthier, happier lives while helping them reach their goals.

We are a group of individuals who are passionate about building the best line of products in healthcare. Every products we are building has a connected experience with all others that we have built. we want to bring the eco-system experiense in healthcare, where healthcare provides and customers are connected in a single environments.

We offer nutrition advice, health-and-fitness tools, a highly engaged and supportive community,and resources that are 100% free. Our proven system inspires, motivates, and supports our members to build healthy and sustainable habits. Far beyond just weight loss, sensebio helps everyone learn to eat better and exercise regularly—for life.

Our Philosophy

SENSE.BIO is an experience driven company.

We thrive to deliver the best connected experience through our products. Our version of healthcare is more connected. Smiple & safer To do this, we work with healthcare providers in the most consolidated manner, to know more about how recoreds are generated , where technology can deliver its best. and patient experience can be improved.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Problems can be solved the easy way or the right way. We price ourselves in the rights way. For healthcare providers, we not only help them work remotely, but also efficiently. We help them visualise their finance and operations with analytics, charts & statistis while maintaining the focus on the potient. For, we help you manage your medical records without you having to do anything. we bring our understanding of healthcare and analytics to help you manage your health better and more easily.
